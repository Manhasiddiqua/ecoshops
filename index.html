Our Vision

EcoShops encourages and strives to rebuild customers shopping habits, make shoppers think twice before buying, and encouraging them to always pick sustainably.

With EcoShops, our website outlines the product's material composition and highlights the sustainable resources used, as well as give a bit of background information about the website and company they are shopping from.

About Us

Hello, we are EcoShops, a website programmed to allow consumers the chance to shop sustainably. Our platform that cross-examines products and links throughout the internet to help users find the most sustainable and eco-friendly option for their wants and needs.

Our team consists of 4 individuals: Manha Siddiqua, Saryu Rawaley, Kavita Goski and Wisdom Walker
